Creator

Douglas Carl Engelbart was born on January 30, 1925 and died on July 2, 2013 at the age of 88. He was an american eventor and became known as a computer genius.
Engelbart created the computer mouse, develope of hypertext, network computers and the graphical interface.
With all of Engelbart creation, he also adapted a 5-key "keyset".
The keyset was used for typing and entering in the commands. The creation of the Keyset, eventually led to the Keyboard.
