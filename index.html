Introduction to Keyset

The Keyset was a device that could help us navigate virtual environments without moving your hands back and forth.
It resembled 5 piano keys, and allows the user to enter different character, commands etc. by pressing keys together.
The keyset was designed to be used by one hand leaving the other one free. The keyset also became popular by its' name "The Chorded Keyboard".
Douglas Engelbart was the first to introduce the keyset to the world as a computer interface in 1968. These portable devices became very popular with the wearable computer movement in the 1980s.
